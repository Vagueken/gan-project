An introduction to GANs




GAN stands for Generative Adversarial Network. GANs are one of the most fascinating innovations in deep learning

It works like this: Two competing neural networks are trained. The first network is trained to generate images, and the other is trained to recognize images.

The first network is called a generator, and the second is called a discriminator.

Here's an example

A generator is trained with cat pictures. It starts to learn from the cat pictures, and generate its own images of a cat.

The discriminator then tries to differentiate between a real cat and the fake cat created by the generator.

The generator starts to come up with increasingly complex images to trick the discriminator. As the generator improves at creating life-like images, the discrimator improves at differentiating.

These networks are trained to compete, and one improves as the other does.
